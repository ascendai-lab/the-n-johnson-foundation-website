### **Summary**

The N. Johnson Foundation is actively supporting the back-to-school season in Belize by providing essential educational resources to students and schools. Founded by Nicole Johnson and co-founded by attorney Monica Stewart, the foundation leverages proceeds from Johnson’s novels—stories focused on resilience and healing—to fund its initiatives. This year, the foundation has directly assisted over 30 students by supplying backpacks and classroom materials, while also awarding scholarships, school bags, and essential supplies to six students. Beyond individual student support, the foundation has extended aid to schools to ensure that both educators and learners are well-equipped for the new academic year. Looking ahead, the foundation aims to broaden its mission by introducing mental wellness workshops targeted at students and families throughout Belize.
